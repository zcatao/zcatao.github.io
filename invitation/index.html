Call for Papers

The 12th IEEE International Conference on Communication Systems and Projects (IEEE ICCSP) will be held on 18-20 December 2018, in Zhengzhou, China. The conference will feature keynote speeches by distinguished leaders in academia and industry, technical presentations, and invited sessions on all topics that interest researchers in the broad area of communication systems, networks, and applications. It is sponsored by IEEE Communications Society Singapore Chapter, IEEE Vehicular Technology Society Singapore Chapter, Information Engineering University, Henan Institute of Electronics, and IEEE Zhengzhou Section

We encourage all researchers to submit their best work to IEEE ICCSP 2018, and look forward to welcoming you to Zhengzhou in December 2018.

Please submit your abstracts(300 words) and papers to the Email lixiangzhi2231@163.com in word or latex format.
 
The topics of interest include, but are not limited to, the following:
