The Registration is open Register Now

Registration is REQUIRED for all participants of The 12th IEEE ICCSP.

  • The 12th IEEE ICCSP Symposium Authors Registration Deadline: Monday, 5 October 2018.
    Authors Must register as FULL or LIMITED. A symposium paper author registration covers up to 3 symposium papers.

  • ICC 2018 Workshop Authors Registration Deadline: Thursday, 8 November 2018.
    A workshop paper author registration covers up to 2 workshop papers.

  • Advance Registration Deadline: Monday, 23 November 2018

Please do not share your registration confirmation code with anyone else. Although the code enables submission of up to three papers, it can only be used once, by you, to create an author account for these submissions.
